You can use the following operations to explore more about the chicken travelling in screen. Follow these steps for the same:

(1) USER BUTTONS ON SCREEN
(a) View Model anticlockway: Chicken takes steps in the anticlockwise direction, to change the it's viewing angle while travelling.
(b) View Model clockway: Chicken takes steps in the Clockwise direction, to change the it's viewing angle while travelling.
(c) Start Again Now (Flex Angle): Chicken starts to flex, from the first position when you click this.
(d) Slider bar: Try the 2 gradient colors on the Chicken, that changes according to position of slider chosen by you.



(2) KEYBOARD CONTROLS
(a) UPPER ARROW: Increases blue saturation in the middle part of the Chicken.
(b) DOWN ARROW: Increases red saturation in the middle part of the Chicken.
(c) LEFT ARROW: Increases red saturation in the right part of the Chicken.
(d) RIGHT ARROW: Increases blue saturation in the right part of the Chicken.
(e) PRESS 'A': Sets the 'red' attribute in the right part to 0.7
(f) PRESS 'D': Sets the 'blue' attribute in the right part to 0.7


(3) MOUSE CONTROLS
(a) DRAG the Egg: Go to the canvas area and click on the egg/nest area that you see
Now you can drag it around and place it anywhere on the screen!
Once you find a position click again there.
